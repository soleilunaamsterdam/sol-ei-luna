Tapas Restaurant and Cocktailbar

De Pijp — Amsterdam

Craving tasty tapas and refreshing cocktails? Welcome to Sol el Luna, your chill spot in Amsterdam for Spanish bites and handcrafted drinks. Think crispy Patatas Bravas, creamy Croquetas, sizzling Gambas al Ajillo and juicy Chuletitas de Cordero. Pair it with a zesty Paloma, smooth Espresso Martini or fruity Watermelon Vibe. Feeling bubbly? Our Sangria (glass or pitcher) is a must! Not drinking? We’ve got mocktails too. Laid-back vibes, great flavors and good times - see you at Sol El Luna!
